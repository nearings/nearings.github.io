Opening Reception
November 8th, 6–10pm

Shanghai Seminary opens at 3262 S. Morgan St., Chicago, IL. Running from November 8th to December 23rd, the gallery’s inauguration presents an installment of two contemporary cultural collectives’1,2 respective video productions revolving two historical revolutionary individuals—the Chinese feminist and reformer Wang Huiwu (1898–1993) and the American minister and activist Martin Luther King Jr. (1929–1968).

1Duan Xin is the Artistic Director at the Jiaxing Center for News and Media (Corporate). She has produced legal, social and biographical television programs such as Lines of Law, Truth & Feeling Online, Interviewing Now, and Life Warm & Cold before the rise and hype of variety shows at Jiaxing Television Station. She is currently directing a documentary on the Hong Kong wuxia novelist Louis Cha Leung-yung aka Jin Yong.

2Our Literal Speed is a text and art undertaking located in Selma, Alabama, since 2006.
